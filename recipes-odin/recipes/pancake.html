Pancakes

Description

Simple recipe for delicious stack of pancakes.

Cover them with your choice of topping.

Ingredients:

  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 2 cups 250ml milk (skim, low fat or full fat)
  • 2 1/4 cups white self rasing flour*

Steps:

  1. Wisk the eggs and milk together.
  2. add in the flour and beat until the batter is smooth
  3. heat a pan over low-medium heat with oil - Remove Excess
  4. Pur 1/3 cup of batter per pancake - cook for 1 to 2 minutes
  5. flip and cook until golden brown
  6. transfer to plate - repeat
  7. Server immediately with your choosen toppings
