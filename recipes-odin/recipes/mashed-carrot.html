Mashed Carrot

Description:

Mashed Carrot is a healthy side dish that goes well with meat

This recipe focuses on simple yet homely style of carrots

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds carrots, about 12 large carrot, scrubbed
  • 4 tablespoons butters, or more to taste
  • 1/2 cups chicken stock
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder

Steps:

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil
  2. Chop the carrots into even sizes
  3. Boil in water for 15-20 minutes
  4. Drain water add carrots back to pot
  5. Add in butter, chicken stock, salt, pepper and garlic
  6. Mash the carrots using a hand masher
  7. For extra smooth carrot use a immersion blender
